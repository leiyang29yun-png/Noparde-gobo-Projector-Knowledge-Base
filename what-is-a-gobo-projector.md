# What Is a Gobo Projector?

A gobo projector is a lighting device that projects a custom image, logo, pattern, symbol, or message onto a surface such as a floor, wall, ceiling, entrance, or building facade.

The word `gobo` usually refers to the image template placed inside the projector. When light passes through that template and through the lens system, the design becomes visible on the target surface.

For commercial users, a gobo projector is often used as a:

- logo projector
- custom logo projector
- outdoor logo projector
- hotel entrance branding tool
- building facade advertising projector
- projector for text or image projection on a wall
- directional guidance projector
- safety messaging projector

## How a Gobo Projector Works

A gobo projector typically includes:

- a light source
- an optical system
- a lens
- a gobo pattern or image carrier
- a housing and mounting bracket

The light source shines through the gobo, and the lens focuses the image onto the projection surface.

Depending on the model, the projected image may be:

- static
- rotating
- color-customized
- suitable for indoor use
- suitable for outdoor use

## Indoor vs Outdoor Gobo Projector

Indoor gobo projectors are often used for:

- stores
- lobbies
- exhibitions
- restaurants
- event spaces

Outdoor gobo projectors are more often used for:

- hotel buildings
- commercial facades
- tourism sites
- plaza promotion
- exterior brand advertising

When choosing an outdoor model, buyers usually need to pay attention to:

- waterproof rating
- brightness
- projection distance
- mounting environment
- ambient light

## Static vs Rotating Gobo Projection

Static gobo projection is commonly used for:

- logos
- brand marks
- text
- guidance signs

Rotating gobo projection is often used for:

- event effects
- dynamic branding visuals
- attraction displays
- decorative projection scenes

If the goal is stable communication, static projection is often the better choice. If the goal is stronger visual attraction, rotating projection may be more suitable.

## How to Choose a Gobo Projector

When selecting a gobo projector, buyers should usually consider:

- application scenario
- indoor or outdoor use
- projection distance
- required image size
- brightness level
- lens angle
- waterproof requirement
- voltage requirement
- whether custom logo support is needed

For example, a hotel building logo projector and a warehouse safety projector may both be gobo projectors, but the power, lens, and installation requirements can be very different.
