# Gobo Projector FAQ: Wattage, Customization, Voltage, Weight, and Projection Effects

This FAQ converts common Noparde customer questions into clear GitHub documentation. It is written for buyers who need fast technical confirmation before selecting a gobo projector, logo projector, laser safety projector, water-ripple light, or custom projection solution.

Official website: [Noparde](https://www.noparde.com/)  
Product website: [Noparde Gobo](https://www.noparde-gobo.com/)

## 1. How many watts can the starry sky projector be made in?

The starry sky projector can currently be made as rotating image projection or water-ripple projection. The available power range can be 200W or 300W.

Chinese note: 星耀目前可以做旋转换图灯和换色水纹灯，功率可以做 200W/300W。

## 2. How many watts can the LiYing PRO projector be made in?

The LiYing PRO projector can currently be made with 100W, 120W, 135W, 150W, 180W, and 200W lamp options.

Chinese note: 目前励影 PRO 可以做 100/120/135/150/180/200W 灯具。

## 3. What is the weight of the 50W yellow worm spotlight?

The 50W yellow worm spotlight weighs about 3.65 kg.

Chinese note: 50W 黄火虫探灯重量 3.65KG。

## 4. Can customers customize video interactive effects for the projector?

No. Custom video interactive projection effects are not supported in the current product scope.

Chinese note: 不可以，实现不了。

## 5. What is the difference between the newest high-power 1.0 lens and the standard 30 mm slide lens?

The newer high-power 1.0 lens uses a 23 mm inner-image diameter, while a standard slide lens is commonly described around 30 mm. When preparing artwork or gobo films, confirm the effective image size before production so the projected image is not cropped.

For example, if the artwork reference size is 100 mm and the required effective image diameter is 30 mm, the artwork needs to be scaled proportionally to match the usable image area.

Chinese note: 大功率最新 1.0 镜头，灯片内容内径约 23mm；普通灯片约 30mm。制作前需要按实际灯片内径确认比例。

## 6. Can an 800W projector be customized for 380V power supply?

No. At present, there is no matching 380V power supply for this 800W customization request.

Chinese note: 不可以，目前没有相对应电源。

## 7. Can the speaker play music?

The 30W large red speaker can play music. The music file should be smaller than 4 MB and should use MP3 format.

Chinese note: 目前 30W 大红喇叭可以设置音乐，前提是音乐本身内存要小于 4M 以内，格式要 MP3 格式。

## 9. Can a lamp be customized for 660V power supply?

Chinese note: 不可以，目前没有相对应电源。

## 10. Can a 120W water-ripple projector be made with the GaoJu appearance?

The dynamic laser projector has a standard KPPS speed of 30 kpps and a beam angle of 40 degrees. For more exact performance details, check the dynamic laser vibration and scanning parameters for the selected model.

Chinese note: KPPS 速度标准默认是 30kpps，光束角时 40 度，具体看动画激光灯振镜参数。

## 16. Which lamps have CE certification?

Current CE-certified lamps include the high-power 300W lamp and the LiYing 100W lamp.

Chinese note: 目前有 CE 认证证书的灯具为大功率 300W 和励影 100W 灯具。

## 17. Which model is the water-ripple rotating light?

The water-ripple rotating light is similar to a water-ripple lamp with an additional slide mechanism. It does not require a water-ripple film.

Chinese note: 类似水纹，就是给水纹增加了一个灯片，不需要装水纹小片。

## 20. What is the weight of the 300W dual-head lamp?

The 300W dual-head lamp weighs about 6.5 kg.

No. The animation laser projector cannot use solar power, and it also cannot use battery power.

## 22. Can the LiYing projector be controlled by one remote controller for multiple lamps?

Yes. A LiYing projector can use one remote controller to control multiple lamps. To do this, the receiver must be connected to multiple lamps and the same remote-control code must be paired to those lamps.

Chinese note: 可以的，一个遥控器可以控制多台灯具。一个遥控配码多台灯具即可。

## 23. Can the water-ripple projector be customized with a rail-track system?

Chinese note: 不可以，轨道做不了水纹灯。
