## Writing Advice

Each page should target one main keyword.

Good:

- one article for one topic
- one FAQ page for one topic cluster
- one application guide for one use scenario

Avoid:

- mixing hotel projection and forklift safety in the same article
- mixing low-voltage spotlight and event decoration in the same article
